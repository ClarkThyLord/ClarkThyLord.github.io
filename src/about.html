How did I start all of this?
When I was around eleven or twelve I set out a goal to program a video game by the end of the summer vacation, and so I did… not. Turns out creating a video game when you have no experience in algebra, design, computer science, or anything other than watching youtube videos is very hard. Nevertheless, I never gave up, and using the one thing I was able to do, watch youtube videos, I learned everything and anything I could. Minutes became hours, hours became days, days became weeks, weeks became months, months became years and by then I was able to develop video games left and right; of course, not the prettiest of games but they were both fun to play and develop!
